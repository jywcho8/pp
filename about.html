About me

Sports Analyst | Data Enthusiast | Golfer

Hi there! My name is Jason, and I am a sports analyst passionate about leveraging data to drive fan engagement, operational efficiency, and business growth in the sports industry.

I completed my bachelor's degree in California, and then joined my family's franchise F&B business in Korea. After spending a few years immersed in operations and marketing, life took me on a different journey—one that aligned more closely with my passion for sports.

Since I was 14 years old, golf has sparked a deep-rooted passion for the sports industry within me. That passion led me to fully commit to a career in sports. I've had the hands-on experience of coordinating international golf tournaments, high-profile pro-am events, and charity events, each deepening my understanding of what it means to connect people through sports.

Currently, I’m pursuing a master's degree in Sports Administration with a specialization in Data Analytics at Northwestern University. I'm focusing on transforming complex sports data into compelling visual stories and actionable insights, particularly in golf and Formula 1. I believe the future of sports lies in understanding audiences better through the power of data.
